ABOUT THE EVENT

The Cricket Auction Competition at EnVision 2K25 brings the thrill of strategy and bidding to the world of cricket. Participants will step into the shoes of team owners, analyzing player stats, managing budgets, and making smart bids to build the ultimate dream team. With a mix of cricket knowledge, strategic planning, and quick decision-making, this competition is perfect for cricket lovers who enjoy the excitement of auctions.

GUIDELINES
  1. The registration fee for this event is Rs.50 per team

  2. Total prize-pool is of Rs. 1000

  3. Each team will comprise a maximum of 3 members and a minimum of 2 members.

  4. Cross-college teams are allowed.

  5. The Auction event will comprise 2 rounds: Preliminary round and Final round.

  6. The preliminary round will comprise 10 questions.

  7. The questions will be based on the topic of International Cricket and IPL.

  8. Top 10 teams will qualify for the Final Auction round.

  9. Each team will have a purse of 60 Crore.

  10. Each team will have to buy a minimum of 12 players and a maximum of 14 players.

  11. A team should have a minimum of 3 Batters, 1 Wicket-Keeper, 2 All-Rounders, and 3 Bowlers.

  12. Among the 3 Bowlers, one bowler should be a Spin Bowler.

  13. A team can buy a maximum of 6 Overseas players.

  14. The evaluation of teams will be based on the overall rating of the playing XI of a team.

  15. The maximum rating of a player is 10.0.

  16. Cross Bidding is not allowed when two teams are bidding among themselves.

  17. Mobile phones and laptops are not allowed in the Preliminary Round.

  18. Mobile phones and laptops are allowed in the Final round.

  19. The decision made by the Auctioneer is considered final.
