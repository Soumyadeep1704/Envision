ABOUT THE EVENT

The Coding Competition at EnVision 2K25 is a battle of logic and programming skills. Participants will solve real-world problems using algorithms, data structures, and creativity. Whether you're a beginner or an expert coder, this competition is the perfect opportunity to test your skills, innovate, and compete with the best minds in tech.

GUIDELINES
  1. Individual Participation.

  2. The competition will take place on the HackerRank platform.

  3. Participants must have a HackerRank ID.

  4. It consists of two rounds, each featuring three questions of different difficulty levels.

  5. Participants who succeed in Round 1 will move on to Round 2.

  6. Difficulty Level: Medium to Hard Programming Languages: Java, C++, C

  7. Any form of code copying or sharing will lead to disqualification.
