ABOUT THE EVENT

The Debate Competition at EnVision 2K25 is a dynamic platform for tech enthusiasts to engage in thought-provoking discussions on emerging technologies, innovation, and sustainability. Participants will debate pressing issues in the tech world, showcasing their critical thinking, research, and persuasive skills. The competition aims to foster intellectual discourse, challenge perspectives, and inspire innovative solutions for the future.

GUIDELINES
  1. Each Team comprises 2 members.

  2. One member will speak for the motion and another member will speak against the motion.

  3. Cross-college teams are allowed.

  4. The debate competition will be held in Oxford Style (languages allowed are English and Bengali).

  5. Each member will have a time of 2 minutes to speak. Then you will get a time of 1 minute for rebuttal questions.Decisions made by the judges are final.

  6. Any kind of misbehavior or misconduct can lead the team to disqualification.
