ABOUT THE EVENT

The Quiz Competition at EnVision 2K25 is an exciting challenge for tech enthusiasts to test their knowledge in science, technology, and innovation. Covering a wide range of topics from AI to cybersecurity, the quiz will push participants to think fast and stay updated on the latest advancements. Get ready to compete, learn, and showcase your tech expertise!

GUIDELINES
  1. Each team should comprise 2 members.

  2. Cross college teams are allowed.

  3. The quiz event will comprise two rounds: Preliminary round Final round

  4. The Preliminary round will comprise 15 questions. There will be 4 star-marked questions which will help to break the tie between two teams.

  5. Top seven teams will qualify for the Final round.

  6. The final round will comprise 3 rounds, where one round will be the Tech round.

  7. The decision of the Quiz master is final.
